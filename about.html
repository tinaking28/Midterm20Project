About Us - Our Story

Our Story

The owners' love of baking began when they started making cakes and cookies for their kid's birthday parties and special events. After several years of sharing treats with family and friends, they opened the doors to Just Baked in March 2020. We bake homemade cupcakes and sweet treats in an inviting, fun, open kitchen atmosphere. Visit any of our locations to enjoy an afternoon cupcake with friends or take a dozen of your favorite flavors to goWhether you're planning a wedding or need a dozen cupcakes for your child's next birthday party, the Just Baked team can't wait to help!
